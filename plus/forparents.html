How I can create an account for my kids?

So.. you can click create, if you don't have an account or signed out, a create account and a log in button wil appear.
Click on create account, if you choose: I'm parents, you can create an account and then choose the manage button, if you created a kids account, type its username and password to manage. If you choose: I'm kids, when you created your account, you can share arts or let your child share its new arts, mind maps , etc.

Teacher

Ok, now is teacher. When you create an account, three buttons will appear, Parents, Kids, Teacher. Why i created this "teacher" button? Teachers is the same of parents, but they can create classes and share their videos to teach your kids, if your kids is in their classes , your kids can do homeworks, ask the Teachers. Teachers can reply the questions.
